WHAT IS THIS

An 8-mission interactive experience that helps youth (ages 10–16) understand AI through games, stories, and real tools.
Learn:
what AI is & where it appear
show AI learns & why bias happens
how AI supports creativity & community
safe + responsible AI use
how to write strong prompt
show to evaluate AI with human judgment
how to imagine human-centered futures
Built with Ampere values: respect, transparency, co-design, human-first.

HOW TO USE THIS RESOURCE

  • Move through the missions in sequence to build understanding step by step.
  • Each mission includes a starter activity, main lesson content, a game, and reflection questions.
  • The missions are organized into sections, with the main content delivered through modular cards. Feel free to pause at any point to discuss ideas, make connections to your lessons, or reflect before moving on.
